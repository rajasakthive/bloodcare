Why Donate Blood?

Donating blood can save lives. It helps those in emergencies, surgeries, and chronic conditions. Your contribution makes a difference.

Who Can Donate?

Most healthy individuals aged 18-65 can donate blood. You must weigh at least 50kg and be in good health.

How to Donate?

Find a nearby blood bank, schedule an appointment, and follow simple pre-donation guidelines like staying hydrated and eating a healthy meal.
